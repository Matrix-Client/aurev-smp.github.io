📜 Server Rules & Regulations

To keep Aurev SMP fun, welcoming, and fair for everyone, we enforce a strict policy on these rules. Failure to comply will lead to a ban:

  • No Cheats / Macros / Hacks / Exploits: Using modified clients, duping items, or exploiting bugs will get you banned.
  • No X-ray: Using X-ray textures, mods, or resources is strictly prohibited. Yes, this includes X-ray texture packs!
  • No CPVP: Crystal PvP, anchors, and bed PvP are disabled/banned. We want to keep regular PvP fun without making other game equipment useless.
  • Do Not Lag the Server: Going out of your way to build lag machines or deliberately hurt server performance will lead to an immediate ban.
  • Don't Spam Chat: Keep the chat clean and readable. Spamming is annoying for everyone.
  • No Discrimination: We have a zero-tolerance policy for racism, homophobia, transphobia, or any forms of hate speech.
  • Respect Personal Boundaries: Do not break personal boundaries or take things from the game into real life (even if you say it is a joke).
  • No Seed Cracking: Cracking the server seed or using external tools like Chunkbase to locate structures is strictly forbidden. Keep the exploration fair!
